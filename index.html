Reminder Setup

  1. Open a goal and enable Set a reminder.
  2. Choose frequency: hourly, daily, weekly, custom, or once.
  3. For weekly reminders, choose both day and time.
  4. Confirm iOS notification permission if prompted.

Widget Setup

  1. Long-press Home Screen and tap +.
  2. Search Goalio and pick widget size.
  3. Select a specific goal for context-aware deep linking.
  4. Use app settings to switch widget appearance theme.

FAQ

Why am I not getting reminders?

Check both Goalio reminder toggle and iOS notification permission in Settings. Low Power Mode and Focus filters can also delay delivery.

Do widgets open the related goal screen?

Yes. Tapping a widget should deep-link directly into the goal represented by that widget.

How is data stored?

Goal data is stored locally on device and can be synced with iCloud when enabled in app settings.
