Goalio ("we", "us", or "our") provides a goal-tracking app for iOS. This Privacy Policy explains what information Goalio processes, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Process

When you use Goalio, the app processes data you enter, including:

  • Goal titles, goal settings, progress values, and completion history
  • Reminder preferences (frequency, time, selected weekday/custom interval)
  • Appearance settings (colors, emoji, optional goal icon photos)
  • In-app preferences (for example, week start day, widget theme)

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide goal tracking features and progress calculations
  • To schedule local notifications you explicitly enable
  • To display widgets and keep widget content in sync
  • To sync data across your devices when iCloud Sync is enabled

3. Storage and Sync

  • By default, goal data is stored on-device.
  • If iCloud Sync is enabled, data syncs via Apple CloudKit tied to your Apple ID.
  • Widget data uses Apple App Group storage shared between app and widget extension.

4. Data Sharing

Goalio does not sell your personal data and does not use third-party ad profiling. Data may be processed by Apple platform services when needed for iCloud sync, notifications, and widgets.

5. Permissions

  • Notifications: used only for reminders you enable.
  • Photo Library (if requested): used only to select a custom goal icon.

6. Data Retention

  • On-device data remains until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • Synced iCloud data remains according to your iCloud account state and deletions.

7. Your Controls

  • Edit or delete goals at any time in-app.
  • Disable reminders globally or per goal.
  • Disable iCloud Sync in app settings.
  • Change permissions anytime in iOS Settings.
