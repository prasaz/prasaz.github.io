Goalio ("we", "us", or "our") provides a goal-tracking app for iOS. This policy explains what data Goalio processes, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Data We Process

  • Goal data you enter (titles, progress, reminder settings, completion data)
  • Optional customization (goal colors, emoji, optional icon photos)
  • App preferences (for example, week start day, widget theme)

2. How Data Is Used

  • To provide goal tracking and progress calculations
  • To schedule reminders you enable
  • To populate Goalio widgets
  • To sync data via iCloud when enabled

3. Storage and Sync

  • By default, data is stored on device.
  • If iCloud Sync is enabled, data syncs via Apple CloudKit.
  • Widget data is shared through Apple App Group storage between app and widget extension.

4. Sharing and Sales

Goalio does not sell personal data and does not use third-party ad profiling. Apple platform services may process data as needed for iCloud sync, notifications, and widgets.

5. Permissions

  • Notifications: used only for reminders you choose to enable.
  • Photo Library (if requested): used only to choose a custom goal icon.

6. Retention and Controls

  • On-device data remains until you delete it or uninstall Goalio.
  • You can edit/delete goals, disable reminders, disable iCloud sync, and change permissions in iOS Settings.

7. Contact
